My Philosophy

I have always believed in a certain kind of code. It's things that we already know and things that we should follow:

  • Treat others the way you want to be treated
  • Always deal with others honestly
  • Always do your best work no matter what you are doing
  • When working on a team, take into account other people's opinions

These core values make up who I am. I love working with other people and being able to work in a workplace where I can bounce ideas off of my teammates. I think the ability to work in a team is the best way to get things done efficiently and effectively.
